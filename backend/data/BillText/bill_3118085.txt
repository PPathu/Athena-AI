  ***AUTHORS SUBJECT TO CHANGE***
Relating to:  proclaiming our appreciation and respect for our Grocery and Retail 
Food Employees.
Whereas, there are nearly 6 million grocery store employees in the United 
States; and
Whereas, in Wisconsin, there are 94,000 grocery store, convenience store, and 
food-related retail jobs that provide $2.8 billion in payroll and generate $6.3 billion 
in economic activity; and
Whereas, grocery, convenience, and food retail employees provide all 
Americans with access to safe, healthy, and affordable food; and
Whereas, grocery, convenience, and food retail employees work to enhance the 
health and well-being of each customer; and
Whereas, grocery, convenience, and food retail employees contribute and 
volunteer countless hours in their communities; and1

Whereas, grocery, convenience, and food retail employees have faced 
unprecedented challenges the last three years keeping retail shelves stocked; and
Whereas, grocery, convenience, and food retail employees have and continue to 
meet and exceed these challenges, while displaying courage, compassion, 
dedication, and leadership, as well as exemplifying customer service and 
community outreach; and
Whereas, the need for grocery, convenience, and food retail employees is 
greater than ever because of current and growing consumer demands as well as 
their reputation for excellence; now, therefore, be it
Resolved by the assembly , That the Wisconsin Assembly hereby proclaims 
our appreciation and respect for our Grocery and Retail Food Employees and asks 
all citizens to join in honoring our Grocery Store Heroes.
