Resolved by the senate , the assembly  concurring, That:
SECTION  1.  2025-2026 session schedule.   (1)  B IENNIAL  SESSION  PERIOD .  
The legislature declares that the biennial session period of the 2025 Wisconsin 
Legislature began on Monday, January 6, 2025, and that the biennial session period 
ends at noon on Monday, January 4, 2027.
(2)  B UDGET  DEADLINE  EXTENDED .  The deadline of Tuesday, January 28, 2025, 
set by section 16.45 of the statutes for introduction of the executive budget bill or 
bills, submittal of the state budget report, and delivery of the governor’s budget 
message, is extended to February 20, 2025.
(3)  S CHEDULED  FLOORPERIODS  AND COMMITTEE  WORK  PERIODS .  (a)  
Unreserved days.   Unless reserved under this subsection as a day to conduct an 
organizational meeting or to be part of a scheduled floorperiod of the legislature, 
every day of the biennial session period is designated as a day for committee activity 
and is available to extend a scheduled floorperiod, convene an extraordinary 
session, or take senate action on appointments as permitted by joint rule 81.
