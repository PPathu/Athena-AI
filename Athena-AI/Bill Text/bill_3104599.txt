  ***AUTHORS SUBJECT TO CHANGE***
AN ACT to create  5.051 of the statutes; relating to:  the suspension of a rule of 
the Elections Commission.
Analysis by the Legislative Reference Bureau
This bill is introduced as required by s. 227.26 (2) (j), stats., in support of the 
action of the Joint Committee for Review of Administrative Rules in suspending 
emergency rule 2407 of the Wisconsin Elections Commission on July 22, 2024.  The 
suspended emergency rule prohibited municipalities from 1) providing absentee 
electors with any uniform instructions for absentee voting other than those 
prescribed by the commission and 2) representing that any additional 
administrative or logistical instructions are the uniform instructions prescribed by 
the commission.
The people of the state of Wisconsin, represented in senate and assembly, do 
enact as follows:
SECTION  1.  5.051 of the statutes is created to read:

elections commission that took effect on June 10, 2024, and that created s. EL 6.05, 1
