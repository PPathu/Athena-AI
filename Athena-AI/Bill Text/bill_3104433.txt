  ***AUTHORS SUBJECT TO CHANGE***
AN ACT to create  50.373 of the statutes; relating to:  pelvic exams on 
unconscious patients and creating an administrative rule related to hospital 
requirements for pelvic exams on unconscious patients.
Analysis by the Legislative Reference Bureau
This bill requires hospitals to ensure written informed consent is obtained 
from a patient before a pelvic exam is performed solely for educational purposes on 
the patient while the patient is under general anesthesia or otherwise unconscious.  
The bill also creates a Department of Health Services rule providing that hospitals 
must maintain written policies and procedures requiring written informed consent 
to be obtained from a patient before a pelvic exam is performed solely for 
educational purposes on the patient while the patient is under general anesthesia 
or otherwise unconscious.
