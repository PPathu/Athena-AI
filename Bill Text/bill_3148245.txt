  ***AUTHORS SUBJECT TO CHANGE***
AN ACT to amend  20.931 (title); to create  20.931 (1) (am) and 20.931 (3) of the 
statutes; relating to:  governmental restrictions based on the energy source of 
a motor vehicle or other device.
Analysis by the Legislative Reference Bureau
Under this bill, no state agency and no local governmental unit may restrict 1) 
the use or sale of a motor vehicle on the basis of the energy source used to power the 
motor vehicle, including use for propulsion or use for powering other functions of 
the motor vehicle, or 2) the use or sale of any other device on the basis of the energy 
source that is used to power the device or that is consumed by the device.

SECTION  3.  20.931 (3) of the statutes is created to read:
20.931 (3)  PROHIBITIONS  BASED  ON ENERGY  SOURCE .  No state agency and no 
local governmental unit may do any of the following:
(a) 1.  Restrict the use or sale of a motor vehicle on the basis of the energy 
source used to power the motor vehicle, including use for propulsion or use for 
powering other functions of the motor vehicle.
2.  This paragraph does not affect any policy established by a state agency or 
local governmental unit related to the purchase of motor vehicles for the use of the 
state agency or local governmental unit.
(b)  Restrict the use or sale of a device other than a motor vehicle on the basis 
of the energy source that is used to power the device or that is consumed by the 
device.  Under this paragraph, a device is powered by an energy source or consumes 
an energy source if any significant function of the device uses that energy source or 
consumes that energy source to accomplish the function.
